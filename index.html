More than 1.25 million people die every year due to road accidents. The Bystander Effect states that individuals are less likely to offer help to victims when other people are present which can prove to be disastrous in road accidents. Hence, the use of technology is imperative to automate this process. AccidentAI is an artificial intelligence-based project developed with the purpose of automating the detection of road accidents and calling for help. It is an object detection model based on Mask Region Convolutional Neural Network algorithm. The model has been trained on a set of images of cars taken from actual CCTV feed. The model highlights the areas where it finds a positive match and draws a boundary around the region in which it detects the match.

There are more than ten thousand massive open online courses(MOOCs). But they are present in only one language - English. English is a language spoken only by 20% of the World’s population. Lingz caters to the remaining 80%. Lingz is a service to translate Massive Online Open Courses(MOOCs) and other video tutorials to different languages. It will make these courses available to a broader audience by providing translated audio and subtitles. It will encourage more people to explore different courses and skills on their own. It has been conceptualised to enable the masses, access to the knowledge they never had before. Lingz will help provide people with a new way to translate education and go beyond the boundaries of the English language. Qualified for the Semi-Finals of Conrad Innovation Challenge 2018-19.

Textbooks and other learning sources tend to be extremely long and wordy. Abridge is a one-of-its-kind service that can be used to annotate voluminous texts and lectures. Abridge eliminates the need for such documents and transcripts and provides short notes for them. It has been implemented using Natural Language Processing libraries. With Speech to Text support, Abridge can also be used by deaf and limb amputated students to annotate lectures. The annotating is done through Google Cloud Summarizing API. Users can download or copy the summarised version of their text for future use too. This technology can be applied in the education, journalism, law and bureaucratic sectors of society. Developed in a team of four for AngelHack 2018.

Asteroid Capture is deemed to be among one of the space industries with massive potential for research and development. In spite of this fact, there have been virtually no attempts at asteroid capture till date. With the discovery of Hydroxyl Groups on the Near-Earth Asteroid Bennu by NASA’s, the demand for an asteroid capture vehicle becomes imperative.SPARK or Space Perturbation Asteroid Recovery Kernel is a research study conducted to find a feasible way to capture, transport and recover asteroids so that they can be researched on the International Space Station. We calculated the trajectory paths, damping velocities, impacter collision energy and methods to transfer it from orbit. Furthermore, we discussed potential problems that might arise and their solutions, with insights into the future of this technology also. I am currently in the process of filing a patent application.

Every year, more than 1.8 million students appear for the Class X CBSE board exam. Everyone from students, parents, teachers, relatives to educational institutions recognises these exams as a critical academic milestone in a student’s life. With the aim to dissect this enigma at the grassroots level, I undertook an exercise to see if there exists a relation between the personality type of a student and his academic performance with the intent of finding a trend in the subject-wise performance. I used the Myers Briggs Type Indicator(MBTI) test for the study and surveyed over 100 students of grades 11 and 12 from my school. I found tangible results which can be used as a tool to augment the study strategy. Moreover, I also mapped the relationship between work ethic and personality types to act as an aid in team developepemt and carry out collaborative initiatives successfully. My research paper was published in the International Journal of Indian Psychology.

Seizures are responsible for putting 50 million people at a high risk of death. GoldenHour is an application that will be run using surveillance cameras in every room of the user. This camera-enabled device processes a real-time video feed using Machine learning models to analyse it for abnormalities like a seizure or fainting and instantly calls emergency contacts if such a threat is detected. Upon successful detection of an emergency, the application will send a notification to the user’s phone to confirm whether or not the result detected is a genuine threat. If the user does not respond within 15 seconds, the app will immediately call the user’s SOS contacts and the nearest medical facility for help. Our technology can also be expanded to the detection of unauthorised presence inside a home and crimes like stabbing or strangling.
